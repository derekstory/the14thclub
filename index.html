Two minutes of calibration, then it's your game

A guided calibration learns how you swing and putt. From then on the game responds to your natural motion — flush it and it flies, get quick and it leaks right, just like the real thing.

Every shot in the bag

Driver off the tee, towering approaches, chips, flops, splash-outs, and a putting stroke with real pace and break. Wind, lies, and elevation change every decision. Cinematic flyovers preview every hole.

Earn your moments

String pars together and light the Afterburner — a flaming bomb downrange. Answer a bogey with a birdie for the Bounce Back. And once a round, the Bullseye paints a three-ring target on the fairway: fly it in for strokes off your card.

Your clubhouse, your crew

Every stat a broadcast booth would read. Friends by club code, head-to-head challenges, a clubhouse leaderboard, and the Weekly event. Interrupted mid-round? Resume exactly where you left off.
